Here are three designers who have inspired her




1.

Erik Johansson

Erik Johansson is a Swedish photographer and visual artist. He is famous for creating surreal worlds through photo combinations. He strives to create realistic but impossible images through his camera and imagination.


He is my most favorite visual artist. His photos always have excellent light and shadow contrasts, using reality as the background to create thought-provoking non-realistic photos. These photos always provide me with creative inspiration and guide me to think about design from different perspectives.

Lauren Hom

Lauren is a designer and font artist born in California. She is particularly outstanding in font design. She likes to use bright colors and playful font graffiti to inject a clean, joyful, and free feeling into her designs. She has collaborated with many well-known companies such as Starbucks, Google, Adobe, etc.


Lauren's works can impress people and make them fall in love immediately. She is always able to decorate words into a beautiful painting, and she can also combine various fonts to create a unified design. The fonts she designs are always very playful, coupled with bright colors, making me feel that the entire design process is joyful. I hope to learn how to make designs look enjoyable.

Kurzgesagt

Kurzgesagt was initially a passionate project after graduating from university. The original intention was to create scientific videos in a way that is not dull and boring, exploring knowledge from a new perspective. It features very bright color schemes, exquisite animated videos, and easy and fun voiceovers, making it the most popular educational channel on YouTube.

It is a YouTube channel that I discovered from videos of my biology classes. It uses vector graphics to create highly detailed and intricate animated videos, combining various bright colors to match perfectly. It is a work that always amazes me every time I watch it. It is very worth learning from.
