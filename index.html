This is Ling Zhou



Ling believes that everything in the world can be an inspiration for design. She loves photography and seeks out the uniqueness of the subject through the lens. She enjoys this process of searching and discovering. She prefers the process of a design being born in her own hands.
Ling grew up in a small family in China and developed a strong interest in painting from a young age. After growing up, she became a designer. She developed her passion for painting into her profession and found happiness and satisfaction in her work.

Then, when Ling immigrated to the United States, her life underwent significant changes as she became a mother of three children. The responsibility of moving to a new country and raising a family has brought her new challenges. Balancing family and personal aspirations is very difficult, so she can only give up her dreams. Just occasionally find a hint of comfort while accompanying children in painting or handicrafts.

As the children grew up and started going to school, she had her own time and went to community college to study. At first, it was just to improve her language skills, but she went to a painting class, which reignited her passion for design and made her want to create with her own hands again. This experience was another turning point in her life, which strengthened her determination to continue her career in design. Ling received support from her family and began to immerse herself in her busy life and studies. Now, she has successfully transferred from community college to a four-year university, getting closer and closer to her dream.

Ling hopes to return to design, not only to revive her career, but also to rediscover herself. She hopes to become a mother who can also make her children proud. She is getting closer and closer on this road now
